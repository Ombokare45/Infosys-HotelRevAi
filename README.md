🏨 HotelRevAI – Hotel Revenue Analysis
Infosys Springboard Virtual Internship 6.0
📌 Project Overview

HotelRevAI is a data analytics project developed during the Infosys Springboard Internship.
It focuses on transforming raw hotel booking data into interactive dashboards and meaningful business insights using Power BI.

The project enables hotel management to:

    Improve revenue performance
    Optimize occupancy rates
    Understand customer behavior
    Make data-driven strategic decisions

🎯 Objectives

    Analyze booking and revenue trends over time
    Evaluate key KPIs like ADR, RevPAR, and Occupancy Rate
    Study customer segmentation and booking patterns
    Identify seasonal demand and cancellation trends
    Provide actionable business recommendations

🛠️ Tools & Technologies

    📊 Power BI
    🔧 Power Query
    📐 DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
    📁 Microsoft Excel

📊 Dashboards Overview

This project includes 4 interactive Power BI dashboards, each focusing on a different business area:
1️⃣ Executive Overview

Provides a high-level summary of hotel performance using key KPIs:

    Total Revenue
    Total Bookings
    Average Daily Rate (ADR)
    Occupancy Rate

📌 Insight:
Revenue is strong, but occupancy is relatively low, indicating unused capacity.
2️⃣ Booking Behavior Analysis

Focuses on how customers interact with booking systems:

    Customer types (Transient, Group, etc.)
    Booking channels (Online, Travel Agents)
    Cancellation patterns

📌 Insight:

    Majority of customers are new (~96%)
    Very low repeat customers (~3%)
    Heavy dependency on third-party booking platforms

3️⃣ Revenue & Pricing Analysis

Analyzes revenue distribution and pricing effectiveness:

    Revenue by hotel type (City vs Resort)
    Revenue by room type and segment
    Pricing trends (ADR)

📌 Insight:

    City hotels generate higher revenue
    Pricing varies significantly across segments
    Opportunity for dynamic pricing

4️⃣ Operational Insights

Focuses on operational efficiency and stay patterns:

    Weekday vs weekend revenue
    Average stay duration
    Seasonal demand trends

📌 Insight:

    Weekday bookings dominate (business travelers)
    Weekend occupancy is low
    Opportunity to improve weekend demand

📈 Key Insights

    ⚠️ Low occupancy despite strong revenue
    🔁 Very low repeat customers (~3%)
    🌐 High dependency on travel agents and online platforms
    🏙️ City hotels are major revenue contributors
    📊 Strong seasonal demand patterns

💡 Business Recommendations

    Improve customer retention strategies
    Promote direct booking channels
    Implement dynamic pricing models
    Increase weekend occupancy through offers
    Reduce dependency on third-party platforms

🎓 Learning Outcomes

    Power BI dashboard development
    Data modeling using star schema
    DAX for KPI calculations
    Business insight generation
    Real-world data analytics experience

        to interactively explore the dashboards and insights
